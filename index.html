About me

I am a CDS Faculty Fellow at NYU Center for Data Science, working on harmonic analysis, spectral theory, convex geometry, and AI for mathematics.

Originally from Barcelona, I got my PhD from UCLA in 2023 under the supervision of Terence Tao, followed by a research internship at Microsoft Research and a postdoc at ETHZ with Svitlana Mayboroda.

My graduate school was supported by a UCLA dissertation year fellowship, Teaching and Research Assistantships, a “La Caixa” post-graduate fellowship, and an ESOP fellowship at ETHZ.
